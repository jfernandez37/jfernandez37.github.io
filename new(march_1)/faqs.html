Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

To book a property, visit the Listings Page, select a property, and follow the booking instructions on the Property Details Page.

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies per property. Please check the individual property listing for specific cancellation terms.

Do I need to create an account to book?

Yes, you need to create an account to manage your bookings and access customer support. You can create an account here.
