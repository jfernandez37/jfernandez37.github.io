Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

To book a property, simply navigate to the property listings page, select a property, and follow the booking instructions.

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies per property. Please check the individual property listing for details.

Do I need to create an account to book?

Yes, you need to create an account to manage your bookings and access customer support.
